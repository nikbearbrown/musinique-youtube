# PEDAGOGY — vox-team-fence-gap

## Learning objective
Viewer understands that individual caution is insufficient for team-level agent safety — the shared space (shared folders, account-level connectors) needs its own explicit governance, separate from each individual's careful personal practice.

## Act structure
- **COLD OPEN (B01):** Confidential contracts unintentionally exposed through account-level MCP connection — no thesis.
- **THE QUESTION (B02):** On-screen question: "Why does individual caution not add up to team safety?"
- **MECHANISM (B03–B04):** Five fenced nodes + unprotected shared center. Mechanism card: "The gap is in the shared space."
- **EXAMPLE (B05):** Dropbox MCP account-level spread — one member adds, all four gain access.
- **PRACTICE (B06–B07):** Two-column compare with and without shared fence. Team checklist: what systems does it touch, one account or all, who owns the decision.
- **RECAP (B08):** Endcard — mechanism sentence.
