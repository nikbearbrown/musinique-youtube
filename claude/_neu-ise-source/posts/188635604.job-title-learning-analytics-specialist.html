Job Title: Learning Analytics Specialist (Part-Time)
Job link: https://www.inferable.app/learning-analytics-analyst-part-time

INFERabe seeks a part-time consultant to support our goal to scale research-validated learner state detectors via the LearningFace.ai open repository and to package detectors for cross-platform interoperable use using international standards.

Responsibilities

  • Predictive & Inference Modeling: Refine and train advanced machine learning models, including Deep Knowledge Tracing (DKT) and Attentive Knowledge Tracing (AKT) models.

    • Select and refine machine learning algorithms that can be modularized and shared.

    • Implement methods for tracking learner engagement and progress through behavioral data.

  • Standardized Data Analysis: Utilize the Experience API (xAPI) and Learning Record Stores (LRS) to process standardized learning data for model training.

  • Model Documentation: Develop comprehensive “Model Cards” to describe a model’s purpose, research basis, training data, and ethical considerations (bias and fairness).

  • Interoperability & Integration: Support our team in packaging models as modular microservices or plugins using the Model Context Protocol (MCP) and web services using international standards and to ensure they are “Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Re-usable” (FAIR).

  • Ethical Oversight: Ensure all analytics and AI implementations adhere to the principles of LEBOK Knowledge Area 8.5 regarding data privacy, security, and the mitigation of algorithmic bias.

Qualifications

  • Learning Analytics Expertise: Deep understanding of the scope and definition of learning analytics for optimizing learning.

  • Technical Proficiency: Demonstrated experience in predictive and inference modeling, including regression, classification, and deep learning algorithms for .

  • Data Wrangling: Ability to clean and manipulate raw interaction logs and transform event data into meaningful learner state data.

  • Standards Knowledge: Familiarity with international technology standards such as IEEE 9274.1.1 (xAPI) and IEEE 1484.20.3 (SCD).

  • Scientific Foundation: Ability to operationalize learning sciences constructs into technical detectors.

Engagement Details

  • Duration: Part-time (possibly full-time in the future).

  • Location: Remote

  • Impact: Your work will help deliver public good modular detectors that help optimize life-long learning everywhere.
