Quantizing weights from −1.5 to 1.5 is harmless. Quantizing an activation from 0.001 to 0.01 with the same scale destroys the signal — 4 095 distinct floats collapse to 2 levels. This video builds the quantization demo with Claude Code, animates the mash on a sine wave, then fixes it with per-channel scaling to recover all 255 levels.
