Remove 20 % of a network's channels and lose under 1 % accuracy. Remove 70 % and it falls off a cliff. The whole game is knowing where the cliff is. This video builds the accuracy-vs-pruning-rate curve with Claude Code, marks the 20–40 % sweet spot, and overlays the unstructured pruning curve to show why it looks identical on paper but buys nothing on real embedded hardware.
