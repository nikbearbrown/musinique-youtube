## Chapter errata (feed back to the book)

- Trivial: the chapter has the model failing "on the morning of day 1,001"; Taleb's text has the feedings end "on the afternoon of the Wednesday before Thanksgiving." Morning vs. afternoon is inconsequential, but if the chapter wants to track Taleb exactly, "afternoon" is his word. The film sidesteps it (no time of day spoken).
- The chapter's turkey passage otherwise tracks Taleb accurately, including the 1,000-day framing (which is Taleb's own figure, "1001 Days in the Life of a Turkey" — a detail worth keeping since secondary sources sometimes misattribute the count).
