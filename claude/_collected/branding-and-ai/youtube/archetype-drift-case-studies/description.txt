Archetype Drift — Three Case Studies with Claude — Nina Harris' Branding and AI course, Chapter 5.

Tropicana. Gap. New Coke. Three canonical cases of brands that drifted from their founding archetype and paid for it. Claude synthesizes the post-mortems into a structured analysis and extracts a drift-signature framework: the early warning patterns that show up before the crisis.

These aren't cautionary tales. They're a detection system.
