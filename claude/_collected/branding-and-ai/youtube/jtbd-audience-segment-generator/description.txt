The real competitor was a banana. Demographics would have separated a 22-year-old grad student and a 55-year-old executive. The job unites them. This tool fires [DEMOGRAPHIC — CONVERT TO JOB] on any segment that leads with age, income, or occupation, and forces the reframe.
