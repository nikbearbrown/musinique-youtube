Build a Brand Metrics vs. Performance Metrics Dashboard with Claude — Nina Harris' Branding and AI course, Chapter 13.

NPS, awareness, consideration — those are brand metrics. ROAS, CAC, conversion — those are performance metrics. They run on different clocks, and mixing them up destroys both. This tool builds a dual-track measurement plan that separates the two and makes the patience gap explicit.

Most measurement failures are category errors. This dashboard prevents them at the architecture level.
