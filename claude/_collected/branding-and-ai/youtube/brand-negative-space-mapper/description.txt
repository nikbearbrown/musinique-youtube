Build a Brand Negative Space Mapper with Claude — part of Nina Harris' course Branding and AI.

Stripe's brand is not what it ships — it's what it consistently declines to ship. Enterprise sales processes: declined for years. Competitor-bashing content: never shipped. Stripe Atlas: 6 years after launch. Issuing: 8 years. Climate: 10 years. Each individual refusal looks like a one-time choice. The 15-year pattern is the brand. This tool maps those refusals, tags each [VERIFIED-PUBLIC] or [INFERRED-FROM-PATTERN], derives a positioning sentence from the pattern, and runs a consistency check.

Prompt used in this video:
  claude "map negative space for [BRAND NAME]:
    Tactical: specific features/categories declined
    tag: [VERIFIED-PUBLIC] or [INFERRED-FROM-PATTERN]
    Strategic: who would this brand stop being to serve them?
    Profile: 2-sentence synthesis.
    Positioning sentence from refusal pattern (not marketing copy).
    Consistency check: COHERENT or flag contradictions."
