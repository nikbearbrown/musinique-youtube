Build an Aaker Dimension Weakness Finder with Claude — part of Nina Harris' course Branding and AI.

David Aaker's diagnostic: find the weakest dimension and intervene there. Intervening on a strong dimension produces little lift. The four dimensions — Awareness, Perceived Quality, Associations, Loyalty — are not equally weighted for every brand. A telecom brand with high behavioral loyalty but near-zero attitudinal loyalty (customers stay because switching costs are high, not because they want to) has a specific and addressable weakness.
