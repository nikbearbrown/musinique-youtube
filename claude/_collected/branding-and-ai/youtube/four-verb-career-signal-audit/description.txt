Build a Four-Verb Career Signal Audit with Claude — Nina Harris' Branding and AI course, Chapter 1.

Ideate. Build. Brand. Ship. Every project in your portfolio maps to some mix of those four verbs. The audit scores each project and reveals which verbs are underrepresented — the gaps in your portfolio that are invisible until you measure them.

Most portfolios are secretly Build-heavy and Ship-light. Find out what yours is actually saying.
