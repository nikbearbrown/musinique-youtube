Build a Signal Collapse Detector with Claude — Nina Harris' Branding and AI course, Chapter 1.

Score every credential in your portfolio on three dimensions: Cost-to-fake, Population-saturation, and Status. When a signal collapses — when everyone has it and it costs nothing — it stops separating you from the crowd. This CLI tool, built on Spence's signaling theory, surfaces which of your portfolio artifacts have already pooled and which ones still do real work.

Credentials don't fail all at once. They erode. This tool catches the erosion early.

Made live in class using the Brutalist video system — a summary of the concepts discussed in today's session of INFO 7375: Branding and AI at Northeastern University.
