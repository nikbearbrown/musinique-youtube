Build a Boondoggle Score Calculator with Claude — Nina Harris' Branding and AI course, Chapter 20.

Decompose any task into what AI does best and what the human should conduct. Score the seam placement. Flag misplaced judgment — the cases where you let the machine decide what the machine shouldn't decide. The Boondoggle Score tells you whether your AI+Human split is actually optimal.

Most AI implementations are fine at 80% and wrong at the 20% that matters. This calculator finds that 20%.

Made live in class using the Brutalist video system — a summary of the concepts discussed in today's session of INFO 7375: Branding and AI at Northeastern University.
