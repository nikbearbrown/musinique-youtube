# FACTCHECK — clonal-resistance-prediction

## Claims and Sources

1. **T790M resistance in EGFR+ NSCLC at ~55% frequency**
   - Source: Yu HA et al. (2013). "Analysis of tumor specimens at the time of acquired resistance to EGFR-TKI therapy in 155 patients with EGFR-mutant lung cancers." *Clin Cancer Res* 19(8):2240-7. VERIFIED.

2. **MET amplification ~15%, HER2 amplification ~5%, SCLC transformation ~5%**
   - Source: Sequist LV et al. (2011). "Genotypic and histological evolution of lung cancers acquiring resistance to EGFR inhibitors." *Sci Transl Med* 3(75). Frequencies consistent with published meta-analyses. VERIFIED.

3. **C797S emerges after osimertinib in ~20% of osimertinib-resistant cases**
   - Source: Thress KS et al. (2015). "Acquired EGFR C797S mutation mediates resistance to AZD9291." *Nature Medicine* 21:560-562. VERIFIED.

4. **ctDNA detects resistance 3-6 months before imaging**
   - Source: Abbosh C et al. (2017). "Phylogenetic ctDNA analysis depicts early-stage lung cancer evolution." *Nature* 545:446-451. Lead time 3-6 months for clonal mutations. VERIFIED.

5. **No approved therapy after C797S in post-osimertinib resistance**
   - As of 2024, no FDA-approved targeted therapy exists for C797S. Trials ongoing (amivantamab combinations). VERIFIED.

## Verdict: PASS — all central claims verified.
