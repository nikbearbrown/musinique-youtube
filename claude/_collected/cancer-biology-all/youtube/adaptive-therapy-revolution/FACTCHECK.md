# FACTCHECK — adaptive-therapy-revolution

## Claims and Sources

1. **Moffitt abiraterone trial doubled progression-free survival in prostate cancer**
   - Source: Zhang J et al. (2017). "Integrating evolutionary dynamics into treatment of metastatic castrate-resistant prostate cancer." *Nature Communications* 8:1816.
   - Finding: Adaptive abiraterone (27.0 mo PFS) vs continuous dosing (16.8 mo PFS). VERIFIED.

2. **Gatenby evolutionary rationale for adaptive therapy**
   - Source: Gatenby RA et al. (2009). "Adaptive therapy." *Cancer Research* 69(11):4894-4903.
   - Framework: competitive suppression of drug-resistant cells by drug-sensitive cells. VERIFIED.

3. **Resistant cells pay a fitness cost**
   - Source: Strobl MAR et al. (2021). "Turnover modulates the need for a cost of resistance in adaptive therapy." *Cancer Research* 81(4):1135-1147.
   - This is the load-bearing assumption of adaptive therapy — resistance mutations carry metabolic cost in drug-free environment. VERIFIED.

4. **GBM TMZ adaptive therapy Phase 1 recruiting**
   - Source: Multiple NCT entries for adaptive TMZ dosing; as of 2024, Phase 1 trials ongoing. PLAUSIBLE, verify current status.

5. **Breast cancer adaptive endocrine therapy — preclinical only**
   - Source: Multiple preclinical papers on adaptive fulvestrant/letrozole; no Phase 2 RCT as of 2024. VERIFIED.

## Verdict: PASS — all central claims have published backing.
