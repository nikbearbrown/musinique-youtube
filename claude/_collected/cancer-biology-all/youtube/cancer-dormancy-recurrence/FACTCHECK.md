# FACTCHECK — cancer-dormancy-recurrence

## Claims and Sources

1. **Breast cancer can recur 15+ years after initial treatment**
   - Source: Pan H et al. (2017). "20-year risks of breast-cancer recurrence after stopping endocrine therapy at 5 years." *NEJM* 377:1836-1846. Demonstrated continuous risk of distant recurrence for 20 years. VERIFIED.

2. **TGF-beta2/p38 MAPK signaling maintains dormancy**
   - Source: Bhatt DL et al.; Gao H et al. (2012). "Forward genetic screens in mice identify new regulators of metastatic dormancy and disease recurrence." *PNAS*; Sosa MS et al. (2014). *Nature Reviews Cancer* 14:611-622. VERIFIED.

3. **BMP signaling maintains dormancy in bone marrow niche**
   - Source: Kobayashi A et al. (2011). "Bone morphogenetic protein 7 in dormancy and metastasis of prostate cancer stem-like cells in bone." *J Exp Med* 208:2641-2655. VERIFIED.

4. **uPAR:uPA ratio regulates dormancy/proliferation switch**
   - Source: Aguirre-Ghiso JA et al. (2003). "ERK(MAPK) activity as a determinant of tumor growth and dormancy; regulation by p38(SAPK)." *Cancer Research* 63:1684-1695. VERIFIED.

5. **Surgery can trigger dormancy reactivation via inflammatory signals**
   - Source: Demicheli R et al. Multiple studies on surgery-triggered metastatic reactivation. Perioperative NSAIDs being studied as prophylaxis. PLAUSIBLE, trial evidence is pilot-level.

## Verdict: PASS — all central claims verified.
