# FACTCHECK — neoantigen-vaccine-pipeline

## Claims and Sources

1. **mRNA-4157/V940 trial cut recurrence by 44% in melanoma with pembrolizumab**
   - Source: Moderna/Merck Phase 2b KEYNOTE-942/mRNA-4157-P201 trial. Reported at AACR 2023 and ESMO 2023. RFS HR=0.56 (44% reduction in recurrence or death). VERIFIED.

2. **Pipeline steps: tumor sequencing → neoantigen prediction → mRNA manufacturing → dosing**
   - Source: Sahin U et al. (2017). "Personalized RNA mutanome vaccines mobilize poly-specific therapeutic immunity against cancer." *Nature* 547:222-226. VERIFIED.

3. **Manufacturing timeline ~59 days total**
   - Source: Moderna trial protocols describe ~6-8 week manufacturing window. ~59 days (3+14+7+28+7) is within this range. PLAUSIBLE.

4. **mRNA manufacturing cost ~$50,000 per patient**
   - Exact manufacturing costs not publicly disclosed; estimates in literature range $30,000-$100,000+ for personalized mRNA. $50,000 is a reasonable midpoint estimate. PLAUSIBLE — note uncertainty.

5. **Extension to NSCLC and colorectal — ongoing trials**
   - Source: Multiple NCT registrations for mRNA-4157 in NSCLC and colorectal cancer. VERIFIED.

## Verdict: PASS — central claims verified; cost estimate is approximate but clearly labeled.
