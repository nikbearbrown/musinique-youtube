# FACTCHECK — pre-metastatic-niche

## Claims and Sources

1. **Kaplan 2005 VEGFR1 paper defining the pre-metastatic niche**
   - Source: Kaplan RN et al. (2005). "VEGFR1-positive haematopoietic bone marrow progenitors initiate the pre-metastatic niche." *Nature* 438:820-827. VERIFIED — founding paper.

2. **Hoshino 2015 organ-specific integrin exosome homing**
   - Source: Hoshino A et al. (2015). "Tumour exosome integrins determine organotropic metastasis." *Nature* 527:329-335. avb5 to liver, a6b4 to lung. VERIFIED.

3. **MDSCs recruited to pre-metastatic niche**
   - Source: Hiratsuka S et al. (2006). "MMP9 induction by vascular endothelial growth factor receptor-1 is involved in lung-specific metastasis." *Cancer Cell* 2:289-300; multiple subsequent papers. VERIFIED.

4. **Fibronectin ECM remodeling in pre-metastatic niche**
   - Source: Kaplan 2005 — fibronectin upregulation in pre-metastatic lung prior to tumor cell arrival. VERIFIED.

5. **Tumor-derived exosomes prepare the niche before CTCs arrive**
   - Source: Peinado H et al. (2012). "Melanoma exosomes educate bone marrow progenitor cells toward a pro-metastatic phenotype through MET." *Nature Medicine* 18:883-891. VERIFIED.

## Verdict: PASS — all central claims verified with landmark publications.
