# FACTCHECK — oncolytic-virotherapy

## Claims and Sources

1. **T-VEC mechanism: modified HSV-1, deleted ICP34.5, added GM-CSF**
   - Source: Andtbacka RH et al. (2015). "Talimogene laherparepvec improves durable response rate in patients with advanced melanoma." *JCO* 33:2780-2788. T-VEC (talimogene laherparepvec): HSV-1 with ICP34.5 deletion, ICP47 deletion, GM-CSF insertion. VERIFIED.

2. **OPTiM trial durable response rate 16.3% vs control**
   - Source: Andtbacka 2015 — DRR 16.3% (T-VEC) vs 2.1% (GM-CSF). VERIFIED.

3. **FDA-approved for melanoma**
   - FDA approved T-VEC (Imlygic) in October 2015 for melanoma. VERIFIED.

4. **Pexa-Vec (vaccinia) Phase 3 failure in HCC**
   - Source: Heo J et al. PHOCUS trial — pexa-vec + sorafenib vs sorafenib alone in HCC. Trial terminated early for futility in 2019. VERIFIED.

5. **Systemic delivery fails due to neutralizing antibodies, liver sequestration, innate immune clearance**
   - Source: Russell SJ et al. (2012). "Oncolytic virotherapy." *Nature Biotechnology* 30:658-670. VERIFIED.

## Verdict: PASS — all central claims verified.
