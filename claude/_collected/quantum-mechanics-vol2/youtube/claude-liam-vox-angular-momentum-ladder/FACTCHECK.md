# Fact Check

- `L_+|j,m>` and `L_-|j,m>` have the same `j` and projections `m+1` and `m-1`, when nonzero.
- Their squared norm factors are proportional to `j(j+1)-m(m+1)` and `j(j+1)-m(m-1)`.
- Nonnegative norms and termination give `m=-j,-j+1,...,j` and multiplicity `2j+1`.
- Ladder algebra permits nonnegative integer or half-integer `j`.
- Orbital angular momentum uses integer `ell` because of the spatial representation/single-valuedness conditions; half-integer angular momentum describes spinorial representations.
- Total magnitude is `hbar sqrt(j(j+1))`.
