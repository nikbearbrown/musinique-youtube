# Fact Check

- `P(a)=|<a|psi>|^2` for a normalized eigenstate is the Born rule; completeness gives a unit sum.
- `|+x>=(|+z>+|-z>)/sqrt(2)`, so each z outcome has probability one half.
- Correction: plus-x and plus-z are 90 degrees apart on the Bloch sphere but the state rays have overlap magnitude `1/sqrt(2)`; the 45-degree picture is the real coefficient plane in the z basis.
- Correction: elementary projection geometry illustrates the Born rule but does not by itself derive or uniquely force the measurement postulate.
- Relative phase can affect later interference even though a single coefficient's modulus squared is phase-insensitive.
