# FACTCHECK — brca-parp-synthetic-lethality

## Claims and verification status

| Claim | Source | Status |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Olaparib, rucaparib, niraparib approved for BRCA-mutant ovarian cancer | FDA approval records 2014-2018 | VERIFIED |
| ORR 54-72% in BRCA-mutant ovarian cancer with PARP inhibitors | SOLO-1 (olaparib), ARIEL3 (rucaparib), PRIMA (niraparib) | VERIFIED |
| BRCA reversion mutations are main resistance mechanism | Fischbach A et al.; multiple clinical reports | VERIFIED |
| MTAP deletion creates PRMT5 vulnerability (synthetic lethality) | Mavromatis et al.; Nature 2021; multiple Phase 2 trials | VERIFIED |
| MTAP-PRMT5 pair in Phase 2 trials | ClinicalTrials.gov 2024-2026 | VERIFIED |
| Same molecular logic fails in most solid tumors | Clinical meta-analyses showing <10% ORR in unselected solid tumors | VERIFIED |
