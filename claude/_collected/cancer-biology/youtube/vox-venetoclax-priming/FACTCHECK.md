## Illustrative numbers summary

All numbers in B11 are ILLUSTRATIVE — labeled as such in narration ("These numbers are illustrative, but this experiment has been done") and confirmed by the chapter's description of BH3 profiling. The directional claims are factually supported.

- Cytochrome c released "within seconds" from CLL mitochondria on BIM exposure: illustrative of high priming
- "Minimal cytochrome c" from normal cells: illustrative of low priming
- Venetoclax "40 nM": plausible therapeutic concentration; illustrative
- CLL cells "dead in 2 hours": consistent with rapid apoptosis following MOMP; illustrative
