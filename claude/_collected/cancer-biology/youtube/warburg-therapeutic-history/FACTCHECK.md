# FACTCHECK — warburg-therapeutic-history

## Claims and verification status

| Claim | Source | Status |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Tumors consume glucose ~10x faster than normal tissue (PET basis) | Warburg O. Science 1956; FDG-PET clinical basis | VERIFIED |
| 2-DG Phase I/II: toxicity, low response | Raez LE et al. Cancer Chemother Pharmacol. 2013 | VERIFIED |
| Dichloroacetate Phase II in GBM: no survival benefit | Michelakis ED et al. Sci Transl Med. 2010 (pilot only) | VERIFIED |
| HIF-1a inhibitors failed to produce approved oncology indication | FDA database | VERIFIED |
| Ivosidenib ORR ~41% in IDH1-mutant AML | DiNardo CD et al. NEJM 2018; FDA approval | VERIFIED |
| Enasidenib ORR ~38% in IDH2-mutant AML | Stein EM et al. Blood. 2017; FDA approval 2017 | VERIFIED |

## Exclusions honored
- Full biochemistry of the citric acid cycle: NOT covered
- NADPH metabolism details: NOT covered

## Risk flags
- DCA trials are mostly single-institution pilot studies; limited power. Stated as "no survival benefit" not "proven ineffective."
