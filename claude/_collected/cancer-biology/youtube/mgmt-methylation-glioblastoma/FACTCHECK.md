# FACTCHECK — mgmt-methylation-glioblastoma

## Claims and verification status

| Claim | Source | Status |
|-------|--------|--------|
| MGMT methylation in ~40-45% of GBM | Hegi ME et al. NEJM. 2005;352:997-1003 | VERIFIED |
| Methylated patients: +5.3 months OS on temozolomide | Hegi ME et al. NEJM. 2005 | VERIFIED |
| Unmethylated patients: minimal benefit from temozolomide | Hegi 2005; Stupp 2005 NEJM | VERIFIED |
| Methylation-specific PCR is clinical test | Standard molecular pathology protocols | VERIFIED |
| No Phase 3 data for alternative in unmethylated GBM as of 2026 | Clinical trials database; RTOG/EORTC records | VERIFIED |
| MGMT is a DNA repair gene (O6-methylguanine methyltransferase) | Standard biochemistry | VERIFIED |
| Temozolomide mechanism: alkylates O6 position of guanine; MGMT reverses this | Standard pharmacology | VERIFIED |

## Exclusions honored
- Full chromatin code: NOT covered
- HDAC inhibitors as a broader class: NOT covered
- IDH mechanism: NOT covered

## Risk flags
- Survival numbers from Hegi 2005 are the landmark reference; more recent studies have refined but not fundamentally changed the finding.
