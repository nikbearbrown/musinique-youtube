# Fact check

VERDICT: PASS

- Chapter values used: Po-212 alpha energy about 8.78 MeV and half-life about 0.3 μs; Th-232 alpha energy about 4.01 MeV and half-life about 1.4×10¹⁰ years.
- Their energy ratio is about 2.19; their half-life ratio is of order 10²⁴.
- Alpha decay is modeled as tunneling through a Coulomb barrier; higher energy reduces the forbidden region and strongly raises transmission.
- Geiger–Nuttall behavior is approximately linear in log half-life versus inverse square root of alpha energy within isotope families; the video says “approximately” and “families.”
- No claim about tunneling time is made.
