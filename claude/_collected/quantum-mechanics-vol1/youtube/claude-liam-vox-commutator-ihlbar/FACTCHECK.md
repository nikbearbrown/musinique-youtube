# Fact Check

- In the position representation, `x_hat psi=x psi` and `p_hat psi=-i hbar psi'` on a suitable common domain.
- `x_hat p_hat psi=-i hbar x psi'`.
- `p_hat x_hat psi=-i hbar(psi+x psi')` by the product rule.
- Therefore `[x,p]psi=(xp-px)psi=i hbar psi`; reversing order gives `[p,x]=-i hbar`.
- Operator composition is not itself a sequence of measurements.
- Robertson gives `Delta x Delta p >= |<[x,p]>|/2 = hbar/2` for states in the appropriate domains.
- The source card's claim that the commutator is the root of every uncertainty relation was narrowed: nonzero commutators underlie Robertson-type bounds, and this commutator gives the position–momentum bound.
