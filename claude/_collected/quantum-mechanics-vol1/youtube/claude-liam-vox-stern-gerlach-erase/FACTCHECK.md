# Fact Check

- `|z+> = (|x+> + |x->)/sqrt(2)` up to phase convention.
- An X measurement of `|z+>` yields `x+` or `x-` with probability 1/2.
- Either X eigenstate yields Z outcomes 1/2 and 1/2.
- A direct repeated Z measurement of `z+` remains certain.
- A nonselective X measurement, or conditioning on either individual X outcome, gives the same final Z probabilities.
- The effect is state update between incompatible observables, not a claim of an uncontrolled classical kick.
