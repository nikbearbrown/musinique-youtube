**The physics:** For a Gaussian pulse f(t) = e^(−t²/2σ_t²), the Fourier transform is also Gaussian with σ_ω = 1/(2σ_t). For σ_t = 1 ps, σ_ω = 5 × 10¹¹ rad/s (≈ 80 GHz bandwidth), and the product σ_t · σ_ω = 0.500 — the minimum achievable by any pulse shape. The simulation verifies two results: the product is exactly 0.5 (minimum uncertainty), and halving σ_t to 0.5 ps exactly doubles σ_ω to 10¹² rad/s.
