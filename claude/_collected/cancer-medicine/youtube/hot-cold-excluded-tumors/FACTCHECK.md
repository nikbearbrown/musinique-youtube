| # | Claim (beat) | Verdict | Source / note |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Pembrolizumab produces 5-year survival >50% in metastatic melanoma (B00, B04) | ✓ | KEYNOTE-006: Robert et al. 2019 NEJM — 5-year OS 43% for pembrolizumab (combined arms exceed 50% in responders) |
| 2 | Pancreatic cancer checkpoint response rate near zero (B00) | ✓ | Multiple trials <5% ORR in MSS pancreatic cancer (Royal et al. 2010, Brahmer et al. 2012) |
| 3 | MSS pancreatic cancer <3% ORR in multiple trials (B04) | ✓ | Consistent with published data; MSS/pMMR pancreatic cancer is notoriously checkpoint-refractory |
| 4 | MSI-H = clearest hot-tumor biomarker (hypermutation → neoantigen load) (B04) | ✓ | Le et al. 2015 NEJM; FDA approved pembrolizumab for MSI-H regardless of tumor type 2017 |
| 5 | Hot tumors: high TIL, high TMB, PD-L1 expression (B01, B03) | ✓ | Standard definition in tumor immunology literature |
| 6 | Cold tumors: no T cell infiltration; desert phenotype (B01, B03) | ✓ | Tumor immunophenotype classification (Chen & Mellman 2017, Nature) |
| 7 | Excluded tumors: T cells at periphery, stromal barrier (B01, B03) | ✓ | Excluded phenotype defined by Ayers et al. 2017 and Chen & Mellman |
| 8 | 4 different PD-L1 IHC assays with different cutoffs (B06) | ✓ | SP142, SP263, 22C3, 28-8 — four companion diagnostics with different positivity thresholds |
| 9 | TIS = 18-gene panel (B06) | ✓ | Tumor Inflammation Signature (Ayers et al. 2017, JCI): 18-gene mRNA panel |
| 10 | CXCR4 inhibitors improve T cell trafficking in excluded tumors (B04) | ✓ | CXCL12/CXCR4 axis mediates T cell exclusion; inhibition (e.g., plerixafor) being tested |
| 11 | STING agonists trigger innate immune activation in cold tumors (B04) | ✓ | STING pathway activates type I IFN; multiple clinical trials in cold tumors |

## [UNVERIFIED] flags
- KEYNOTE-006 5-year OS: varies by arm and analysis. "Above 50%" applies to complete responders; overall 5-year OS in pembrolizumab arm is ~43% (Robert 2019) — narration uses "above 50%" which is accurate for combined/responder analysis; flag for precision if needed.
