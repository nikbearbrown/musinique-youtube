# Fact check

- Po-212 alpha energy is about 8.95 MeV and half-life about 0.3 microseconds; Th-232 alpha energy is about 4.08 MeV and half-life about 14 billion years.
- Leading WKB transmission is exponential in minus twice the forbidden-region action.
- Doubling a rectangular barrier width squares the leading exponential transmission estimate, while prefactors may change.
- For a 4 eV energy deficit, kappa is about 1.02 inverse angstrom; at 5 angstroms, exp(-2 kappa L) is about 4e-5.
- The four applications share exponential barrier sensitivity, not an identical full rate formula.
