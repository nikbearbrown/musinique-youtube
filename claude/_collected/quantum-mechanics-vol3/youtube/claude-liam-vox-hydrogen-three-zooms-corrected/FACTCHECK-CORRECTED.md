# Corrected Thesis

Hydrogen's apparent `n=2` degeneracy resolves on successively finer, explicitly rescaled energy axes: relativistic fine structure separates `j=3/2` from `j=1/2` by about `4.5×10^-5 eV`, while radiative QED lifts the remaining `2S1/2−2P1/2` degeneracy by about `4.4×10^-6 eV`. The second zoom needs about 75,000× the coarse energy resolution; the third needs another roughly 10×.
