# FACTCHECK — psma-theranostic-loop

## Claims and verification status

| Claim | Source | Status |
|-------|--------|--------|
| VISION trial: Lu-177-PSMA-617 improves OS in PSMA-positive mCRPC | Sartor O et al., NEJM 2021 | VERIFIED |
| VISION trial OS: 15.3 vs 11.3 months | Sartor O et al., NEJM 2021 | VERIFIED |
| Pluvicto (lutetium vipivotide tetraxetan) FDA approval March 2022 | FDA approval records | VERIFIED |
| PSMA is a membrane-facing glutamate carboxypeptidase (not intracellular) | Perner S et al.; PSMA biology papers | VERIFIED |
| Salivary gland uptake as primary toxicity concern for PSMA radioligands | Violet J et al.; multiple PSMA therapy papers | VERIFIED |
| Ac-225 alpha range ~50 µm (5-10 cell diameters) | Alpha particle physics; Morgenstern et al. | VERIFIED |
| Lu-177 beta range ~1-2 mm | Beta particle physics; established dosimetry | VERIFIED |
| DOTATATE pair: Ga-68-DOTATATE imaging + Lu-177-DOTATATE (Lutathera) therapy | NETTER-1 trial, Strosberg J et al., NEJM 2017 | VERIFIED |

## Exclusions honored
- Full radiochemistry of radiolabeling: NOT covered
- Dosimetry calculations: NOT covered
- Complete clinical management of PSMA-positive mCRPC: NOT covered
- Photodynamic therapy: NOT covered

## Risk flags
- OS numbers are from the VISION trial ITT population; PSMA-positive selection required.
- Ac-225 theranostic imaging counterpart: no established clinical pair yet — note ongoing development.
