## Exclusions honored
- Full chemistry of nanoparticle synthesis: NOT covered
- Comprehensive list of clinical nanoparticle failures: NOT covered

## Risk flags
- 0.7% is median across heterogeneous study types; some tumor types show higher EPR — state clearly this is the median across all.
- Doxil efficacy vs free doxorubicin: non-inferior, not superior — important distinction.
