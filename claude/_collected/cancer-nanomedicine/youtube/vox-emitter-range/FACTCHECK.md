## Flagged item for narration fix (B06)

The claim "ten to twenty cell lengths" is a factual understatement. At ~10-20 micrometer cell diameter and 1-2mm beta range:
- Low end: 1000um / 20um = 50 cell widths
- High end: 2000um / 10um = 200 cell widths

**Fix for B06 narration:** "That crosses dozens to a hundred cell lengths." This is accurate without being distracting. The specific numbers are not on screen and the key concept (many cells irradiated) is preserved.

> NOTE: The illustrative numbers in B11 (78%, 41%) are from the card's example seed. They are labeled "illustrative numbers" in the viz notes and "illustrative" in narration. They represent a plausible scenario for the mechanism illustration; they are NOT from a published clinical trial and should NOT be presented as such.
