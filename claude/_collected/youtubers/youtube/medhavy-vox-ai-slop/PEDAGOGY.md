## What this reel teaches
AI hallucinations do not simply disappear — they enter the internet as published content, get indexed as credible sources, and get cited by future AI systems. This feedback loop can embed errors into model weights themselves. Provenance verification — tracing facts to primary human-produced sources — is the essential countermeasure.

- Countermeasure: provenance chain must terminate at primary source ✓
