## What the learner already knows
Standing waves, boundary conditions, wavelength, the idea that only certain waves fit a bounded space.

## What this reel teaches
The 1993 IBM quantum corral: 48 iron atoms form a circular boundary on copper. Copper surface electrons can only exist as standing wave modes inside the ring — the iron atoms force ψ=0 at the boundary. The STM image shows |ψ|² as concentric rings. Pattern emerges from boundary + quantum mechanics; no atoms were placed inside.
