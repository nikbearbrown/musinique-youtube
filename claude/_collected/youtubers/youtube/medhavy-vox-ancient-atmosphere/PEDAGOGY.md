## What the learner already knows
Fractions, atmospheric pressure, the idea of diffusion through a tube

## What this reel teaches
pO₂ = f × P (oxygen fraction × atmospheric pressure) controls how deep oxygen diffuses through insect tracheae. Late Carboniferous: f≈0.35, P≈1.05 atm → pO₂≈0.37 atm (75% higher than today's 0.21). This allowed griffinflies with 70 cm wingspans. When O₂ fell, giant insects collapsed. Experimental confirmation: modern dragonflies grow larger in O₂-enriched air.

## Key facts
- pO₂ = f × P ✓
- Today: f=0.21, P=1.0 → pO₂=0.21 atm; max dragonfly 15 cm ✓
- Carboniferous: f≈0.35, P≈1.05 → pO₂≈0.37 atm (75% higher) ✓
- Griffinflies: 70 cm wingspan ✓
- Fossil insect size tracks atmospheric O₂ through geological time ✓
