## What this reel teaches
Antibiotic resistance has a metabolic cost. Bacteriophage predation pressure selects for phage resistance, which bacteria often achieve by dropping antibiotic resistance. This enables a two-step phage + antibiotic therapeutic strategy.

## Key facts
- Resistance is metabolically costly ✓
- Under phage pressure: bacteria trade antibiotic resistance for phage resistance ✓
- 2016 clinical case: Pseudomonas cleared via phage → reversion → antibiotic ✓
- Colistin resistance (2015) shows same trade-off pattern ✓
