# PEDAGOGY — medhavy-vox-energy-ladder

## What the learner already knows
Energy levels, harmonic oscillator, operators acting on a state.

## What this reel teaches
Why the quantum harmonic oscillator has equally spaced energy levels: the raising
and lowering operators (a†, a) each shift energy by exactly ℏω; the ladder must
have a bottom because energy is always positive; solving for the ground state gives
E₀ = ℏω/2 (not zero), the zero-point energy required by the uncertainty principle.
The full spectrum is Eₙ = (n+½)ℏω — uniform spacing, floor above zero.
