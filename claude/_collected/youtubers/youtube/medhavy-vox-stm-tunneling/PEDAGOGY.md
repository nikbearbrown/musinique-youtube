## What this reel teaches
Tunneling current falls exponentially with gap distance (I ∝ e^{−2κd}), making a scanning tunneling microscope exponentially sensitive to gap changes. One ångström closer gives ~10× more current. This steepness means the single closest tip atom dominates the signal, giving atomic-scale resolution.

## Physics gate numbers
- κ = √(2mφ)/ℏ ≈ 1 Å⁻¹ for φ ≈ 4 eV ✓ (ch. 06)
- 1 Å change in gap → factor ~e^2 ≈ 7–10 in current ✓
- Single closest atom contributes 7–50× more than next-nearest atoms ✓
