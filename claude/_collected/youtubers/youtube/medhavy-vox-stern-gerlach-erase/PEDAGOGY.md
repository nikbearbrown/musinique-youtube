## What the learner already knows
Spin up/down, superposition, measurement collapse.

## What this reel teaches
Why inserting a sideways Stern-Gerlach measurement between two vertical ones
destroys the certainty established by the first: spin-up is an equal superposition
of spin-left and spin-right, so the sideways measurement collapses the atom into
a definite sideways state; that sideways state is then an equal superposition of
spin-up and spin-down, erasing the vertical information. The up-down and sideways
spin operators do not commute — knowing one randomizes the other.
