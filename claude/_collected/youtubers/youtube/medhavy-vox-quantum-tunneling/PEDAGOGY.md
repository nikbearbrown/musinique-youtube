## What the learner already knows
Wavefunction, classically forbidden region, exponential decay.

## What this reel teaches
Why a quantum particle can tunnel through a classically forbidden barrier: the
wavefunction decays exponentially inside the barrier rather than stopping at zero;
if the barrier is thin enough, nonzero amplitude survives to the far side and
launches a transmitted wave. Transmission falls as e^{-2κL}.
