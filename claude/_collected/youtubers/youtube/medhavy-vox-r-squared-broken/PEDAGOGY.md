- Bias-variance tradeoff: sweet spot at optimal predictor count
- Best subset: 2^P models (infeasible above P≈40: 2^10=1024, 2^40>1 trillion)
- Backward stepwise: P+1 candidates by greedy removal of least useful predictor
- CV / AIC / BIC to choose among P+1 candidates
