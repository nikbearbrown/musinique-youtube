## What this reel teaches
Why a person walking through a doorway doesn't diffract: λ = h/p applies to
everything, but for a 70 kg person at 1 m/s the wavelength is ~10⁻³⁵ m — twenty
orders of magnitude smaller than a proton. No slit exists that could reveal the
fringe structure. Quantum mechanics is exact for macroscopic objects; the wave
is simply undetectable. C60 buckyballs (λ≈2.5pm) diffract through 50nm gratings
— the boundary between visible and invisible quantum behavior is purely about
available slit technology.
