## What this reel teaches
In a harmonic potential, a special Gaussian wave packet called the coherent state oscillates at the classical frequency ω without ever spreading. The restoring force exactly cancels dispersion. Its width stays frozen at the minimum-uncertainty value σ=√(ℏ/2mω) for all time. Laser light is a coherent state of the electromagnetic field.
