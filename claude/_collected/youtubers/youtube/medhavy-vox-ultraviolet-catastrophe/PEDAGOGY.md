# PEDAGOGY — medhavy-vox-ultraviolet-catastrophe

## What the learner already knows
Wavelength/frequency, energy, the idea of counting wave modes.

## What this reel teaches
Why a hot blackbody emits a finite, peaked spectrum rather than infinite energy
at short wavelengths: the Rayleigh-Jeans law assigns equal energy k_BT to every
mode, predicting infinite total energy (the ultraviolet catastrophe); Planck's
quantization requires each mode to receive a full quantum hν before it can
contribute, starving high-frequency modes when hν >> k_BT and bending the curve
back to zero at short wavelengths.

## Physics gate numbers
- Rayleigh-Jeans: energy per mode = k_BT → infinite total ✓ (ch.01)
- Planck: minimum entry per mode = hν; modes with hν >> k_BT stay empty ✓
- Planck curve peaks and falls; matches all blackbody measurements ✓
- Hotter box → peak shifts to shorter wavelength ✓
- B09: T=3000K, k_BT≈0.26eV; hν(600nm)≈2.1eV → visible modes nearly empty (illustrative) ✓
