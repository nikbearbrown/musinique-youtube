Doppler effect, spectral lines, basic orbital mechanics

## What this reel teaches
The Doppler formula Δλ/λ₀ = v/c encodes the velocity of any light-emitting source directly in the wavelength shift of known spectral lines. Andromeda's blueshift reveals it is approaching at ~110 km/s, heading for a merger with the Milky Way in ~4.5 billion years. Spiral galaxies viewed edge-on show split spectra — one edge blueshifted, one redshifted — yielding rotation curves. Flat rotation curves at large radii demand mass far exceeding the visible disk. Fritz Zwicky applied this to galaxy clusters in 1933 and coined "dunkle Materie" — dark matter — from the excess mass revealed by galaxy Doppler velocities within clusters.
