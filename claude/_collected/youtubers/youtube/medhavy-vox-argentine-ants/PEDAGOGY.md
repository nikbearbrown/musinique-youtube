# PEDAGOGY — medhavy-vox-argentine-ants

## What the learner already knows
What a colony is, genetic diversity, the concept of a founding bottleneck

## What this reel teaches
Argentine ants recognize nestmates via cuticular hydrocarbon chemical signatures derived from genetics. High diversity in native range → diverse signatures → inter-colony aggression. A founding bottleneck (few queens on cargo ships) → low diversity → similar signatures → kin-recognition cannot distinguish distant colonies → global supercolony spanning California, Spain, Japan, Australia.

## Key facts
- Native range: colonies 200m apart fight immediately ✓
- Genetic bottleneck: few queens on 19th century cargo ships ✓
- Invasive range: California and Spain workers show no aggression; mutual grooming ✓
- Supercolony: California, Spain, Japan, Australia act as one colony ✓
- No central coordination — emergent from local chemical recognition rule ✓
