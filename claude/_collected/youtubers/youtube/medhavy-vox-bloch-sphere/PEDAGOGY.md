## What the learner already knows
Qubit as α|0⟩+β|1⟩, superposition, normalization, complex numbers with real and imaginary parts.

## What this reel teaches
A qubit's four real parameters reduce to two angles via normalization and global-phase removal. Those two angles place the qubit state on the Bloch sphere — a single geometric object that contains every possible qubit state. The equator holds equal superpositions; opposite poles are orthogonal states.

## Physics gate numbers
- 4 real parameters → −1 (normalization) → −1 (global phase) → 2 free angles ✓ (ch. 10)
- cos(θ/2)|0⟩ + e^{iφ}sin(θ/2)|1⟩ — Bloch parameterization ✓
- North pole θ=0 → |0⟩; south pole θ=π → |1⟩; equator θ=π/2 ✓
