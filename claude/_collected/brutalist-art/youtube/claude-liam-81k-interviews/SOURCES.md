# SOURCES — What 81,000 People Want from AI

## Primary source

**Anthropic. "What 81,000 People Want from AI." March 18, 2026.**

All data, figures, and quotations in this video are drawn from this single source.
The study was conducted using Anthropic Interviewer (a version of Claude) interviewing
80,508 people across 159 countries in 70 languages.

## Caveats stated in the video (B12)

- These are active Claude users, not a random population sample.
- Early adopters — likely more favorable baseline than general public.
- Hopes were asked before fears, which may inflate hope responses.
