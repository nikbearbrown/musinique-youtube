What is Brutalist? — the first video in "Brutalist: Claude for Video Production."

Anyone can ask an AI to make a whole video in one click, and most of the time what comes back is slop — clean, rendered, and dead on arrival. This video argues why, and what to do instead: you are the conductor. You decide what the piece is and you listen for the wrong note; the machine — an extraordinary player — performs every part on request, one beat at a time. The score is yours; the playing is its.

Every video in this series is made BY the toolkit it describes, and about it — so you can rebuild each one and then point it at your own ideas.

A note on this cut: you'll see a small grey "review label" in the corner of every shot. That's intentional. This is the review cut, and the video explains what that label is and why it's normally stripped from the final — it's left in here so you can watch the machinery while it's being explained.
