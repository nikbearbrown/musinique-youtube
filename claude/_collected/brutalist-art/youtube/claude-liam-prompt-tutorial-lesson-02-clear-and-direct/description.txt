Vague prompts produce vague outputs. That is not a model limitation — it is a specification failure. Claude is a literal instruction-follower. If you leave room for interpretation, it will interpret.

Specificity has four dimensions: length, format, audience, and required components. Each unspecified dimension is a degree of freedom for Claude to fill with its default. Default may not be wrong — but it may not be what you needed.

Treat Claude like a capable new employee who has no context. Tell them explicitly. Write the brief.
