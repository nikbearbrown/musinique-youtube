## The shape of the answer

Metered pricing scales with how much you talk.
Flat pricing doesn't.

A series that generates 3,000–4,000 chars per video (typical for a 3-minute explainer) spends
~$0.21–$0.93 per video on ElevenLabs depending on quota state. That's $2.10–$9.30 per 10 videos.
At flat Suno pricing, 10 videos = 10 generations regardless of word count.

**The break-even is the question.** Once Suno credits per generation × 10 videos cost less than
the EL metered equivalent, Suno wins on price. EL wins on workflow (zero human steps) and
consistency (no speak-song risk).
