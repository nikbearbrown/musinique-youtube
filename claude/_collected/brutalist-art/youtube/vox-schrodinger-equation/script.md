**B02 · CARD** — THE SCHRÖDINGER EQUATION / how the wave function moves
> Last film: psi squared gives the odds. This film: the equation that moves
> psi. Schrödinger's equation — the law of motion for the quantum world.

**T01–T03 · equation tangent — iℏ ∂Ψ/∂t = ĤΨ** (zones 2→3→4; word-keyed spotlights)
> T01: The left side: how the wave function is changing, right now. The
> right side: the energy operator's verdict on the state. Energy drives all
> change.
> T02: i makes change into rotation, not decay. H-bar sets the clock speed.
> And the Hamiltonian — the energy operator — decides how fast each piece
> spins.
> T03: Separate space from time: every energy state becomes a spinning
> phase, turning at energy over h-bar. One electron-volt: two hundred forty
> trillion turns a second.

**B04 · GRAPHIC** — the fork (separation of variables, fig 4.1 as motion)
> The trick is one guess: maybe space and time separate. They do. The
> equation splits — a trivial clock in time, an eigenvalue problem in space.

**B05 · GRAPHIC** — THE CLOCK AND THE SHADOW (the film's visual thesis)
> Each energy state is a clock: real part, imaginary part, spinning
> together. The probability is the clock's shadow. The clock spins. The
> shadow never moves.

**B06 · STILL·ai** — the laser cavity (PROMPT LAW prompt)
> You've seen this physics before — inside every laser. Two mirrors, and
> only the waves that fit exactly between them survive. Everything else
> destroys itself.

**B07 · GRAPHIC** — modes that fit → the menu of energies
> Confinement plus wave equation equals a menu. Only certain shapes fit the
> walls; each shape carries its own energy. That menu is quantization.

**B08 · GRAPHIC** — superposition: the shadow moves
> Mix two states and the spell breaks: the shadow moves. The blend sloshes
> at the beat between their energies — while the total energy holds
> perfectly still.
