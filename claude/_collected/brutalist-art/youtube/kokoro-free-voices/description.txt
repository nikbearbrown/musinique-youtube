Kokoro-82M is a free, local, Apache-licensed text-to-speech model with ~28 named preset voices — each graded by the pack itself (A- down to D-). No API. No meter. No bill.

In this video THE CAST CARRIES IT: Bear's ElevenLabs clone opens, then eight named Kokoro voices introduce themselves and each teach one lesson — what it is, how it plugs into the same interface, the honest grade range (including the F+ voice), the casting field, the full roster, the previz use case, the no-cloning tradeoff, and the D- wink.

This is the third voice engine in the toolkit, after ElevenLabs (metered) and Suno (flat). Who speaks is taste — and taste is yours.
