Installs, .env & Credentials — video 2 of "Brutalist: Claude for Video Production."

Set up once, then make videos. This one covers the parts people actually get stuck on: where keys live (one .env file — a blank key just switches off the feature that needs it, nothing breaks), the per-channel credentials folder, what npx and pip and virtual environments are and why the toolkit uses them, how Claude Code uses the venv, and what the paid services actually do.

And the single highest-leverage thing you can do: record about 30 minutes of your own voice, once, and clone it. Narrate live and you pay a mic-and-room tax every session; the clone moves that cost to once, and every video after narrates itself in your voice, straight from the beat sheet.
