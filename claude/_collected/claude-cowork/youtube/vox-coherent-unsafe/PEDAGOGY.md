## One mechanism

The reel teaches exactly one mechanism: **coherence is not safety** — fluent prose and numbered steps describe a plan's *form*, not its *safety*. A step can be well-phrased and irreversible at the same time. The safety question (backup? dry run? review?) is orthogonal to what makes prose fluent, so the missing safety step is invisible in the format.
