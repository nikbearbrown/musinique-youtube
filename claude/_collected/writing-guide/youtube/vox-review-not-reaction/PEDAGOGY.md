## Practice present (the takeaway law)

B11: Kakutani's diagnostic — evaluate against what the work set out to do, not what you wish it had.
B12: The diagnostic sentence — "The criteria I am using are — and they apply because the work belongs to —" — complete this before drafting.

Both are CONCRETE and CHECKABLE. "Complete this sentence before drafting" is a testable action. "Evaluate against what the work set out to do" is a testable analytical move. Neither is vague caution.
