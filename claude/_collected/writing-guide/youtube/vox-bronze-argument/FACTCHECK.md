## Note on installation date

The chapter text reads "From 1939 to 2020." Some sources (AMNH) record the statue's dedication as October 27, 1940. The discrepancy (1939 vs. 1940) likely reflects completion/commission vs. public dedication. The chapter text is the authoritative source for this reel. Beat B02 narration should say "Installed in 1940" or be changed to "on view from 1939" to match the chapter. Safest: use the chapter's framing — "eighty-one years" — rather than a specific year in narration.
