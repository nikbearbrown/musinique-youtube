## Color law

`TEAL` (plain ink `#2A1A0E`) = the analytical reading — what the composition argues, what the formal choices do. `CRIMSON` (`#C8102E`) = the thing being scrutinized — the compositional hierarchy as argument, the naive surface reading.

Palette: `teardown` (white ground, ink text, red-only accent). Never swap. GOLD = single fill highlight, never text.
