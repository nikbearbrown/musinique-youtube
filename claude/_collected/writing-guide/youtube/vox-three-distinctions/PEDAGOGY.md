## THE PRACTICE check (the takeaway law)

B16: THREE CONCRETE DIAGNOSTIC QUESTIONS:
1. "What is the mechanism?"
2. "Am I invoking a proximate or a distal cause — and does my argument need the other kind?"
3. "Is this cause necessary, sufficient, or one factor among several?"

These are checkable heuristics, not vague cautions. "Be careful" / "keep this in mind" do NOT appear. ✓

B17: Frames the practice payoff — "pass all three and the causal claim holds. Fail one and you know exactly what to fix." Concrete and actionable. ✓
