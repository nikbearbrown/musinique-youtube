### Evidence Integrity Notes

- The four-tier framework is a pedagogical construct, not a citation. It is well-grounded in the AI capability literature but the tier numbering and labels are the series' own framing.
- "Irreducible" is a strong word — the episode treats it correctly as "irreducible today and for the foreseeable future" without claiming it will never change.
- "Distrust-calibration" is a neologism in this context; the underlying concept (automation complacency, appropriate trust) is documented in human factors research.
