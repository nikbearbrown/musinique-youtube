## What the season deliberately does NOT claim

- No invented engagement numbers, conversion rates, or "algorithm formulas."
- No ranking of AI models' creative quality.
- No claim that any recipe grows followers by N% — the season sells hours back
  and scams avoided, not growth guarantees. That's the difference between us
  and the people we're grading.
